## Overview
This dashboard provides a single-page view of ecommerce sales performance, focusing on revenue, profit, transactions, returns, product performance, and regional contribution.

## Business Objective
- Track current month revenue, profit, and transactions
- Identify top-performing products and brands
- Monitor return rate and its impact on profit
- Compare regional sales contribution
- Analyze revenue trends over time

## Dataset
- Ecommerce transactional data
- Includes orders, revenue, profit, returns, products, regions, and dates

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Transactions
- Return Rate
- Product-wise Profit Margin
- Region-wise Sales Contribution
- Monthly and Weekly Revenue

## Key Insights
- Revenue and profit are driven by a small group of high-performing products.
- Some products show high margins but also higher return rates.
- Sales are concentrated in specific regions, indicating priority markets.
- Revenue shows steady growth with seasonal variation.

- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
