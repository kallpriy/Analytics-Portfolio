## Overview
This project analyzes marketplace sales performance for a single month (April 2022). The dashboard focuses on sales volume, revenue, discounts, margins, and product-level performance to understand short-term business behavior.

## Business Objective
- Track sales and order performance for the month
- Understand how discounts affect margins
- Identify top products, brands, and categories
- Compare city-wise order contribution
- Monitor daily sales trends

## Dataset
- Source: Public ecommerce sales dataset (Kaggle)
- Time Period: April 2022
- Data Includes: Orders, net revenue, discounts, margins, products, brands, cities

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales
- Net Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Average Discount %
- Average Margin %
- Total Products
- Total Brands

## Analysis Performed
- Daily net revenue trend analysis
- Product and category-level sales comparison
- Brand demand analysis
- City-wise order distribution
- Discount vs margin relationship analysis

## Key Insights
- Sales are concentrated among a small set of products and brands.
- Higher discounts do not always translate to higher margins.
- Few cities contribute the majority of total orders.
- Margin stability is better in categories with controlled discounting.
- Sales show visible daily fluctuations within the month.

## Tools Used

## Outcome
- Built a focused one-month sales analysis dashboard
- Identified margin risks linked to discount strategies
- Highlighted key products, brands, and cities driving sales
