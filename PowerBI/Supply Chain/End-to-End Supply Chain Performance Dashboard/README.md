## Overview
This project provides an end-to-end view of supply chain performance covering sales demand, inventory health, supplier risk, logistics efficiency, and customer fulfillment. The dashboard enables operational monitoring and decision-making across the supply chain lifecycle.

## Business Objective
- Monitor revenue and demand trends
- Track inventory health and replenishment risks
- Evaluate supplier performance and lead times
- Measure logistics efficiency and OTIF performance
- Understand customer segment contribution and profitability

## Dataset
- This dataset is a synthetic dataset generated for analytical practice and portfolio demonstration purposes.
- Data Includes: Orders, revenue, inventory, suppliers, warehouses, carriers, lead time, OTIF, margins
- Scope: Multi-period operational data

## Key Metrics
- Revenue & Revenue Growth
- Gross Margin %
- Inventory Turns
- Stockout Risk
- Average Lead Time
- On-Time Delivery %
- OTIF %
- Fill Rate %
- Average Days to Ship

## Analysis Performed
- Revenue MTD vs QTD vs YTD comparison
- Demand distribution by region and category
- Inventory levels vs reorder point analysis
- Supplier lead time and reliability assessment
- Warehouse-wise reorder and inventory turns analysis
- Carrier OTIF and SLA variance analysis
- Customer segment contribution and profitability analysis

## Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated in a few regions and categories, creating demand imbalance.
- Inventory risk exists in fast-moving categories despite acceptable overall turns.
- Supplier lead time variability directly impacts service levels and margins.
- OTIF performance varies significantly by carrier and warehouse.
- Retail customers contribute the highest revenue but with tighter margin pressure.
